About Innovista

Building a seamless connection between vision and craftsmanship.

Our Story

Innovista was born from a simple observation: finding reliable, high-quality interior design and restoration services in the Northern Province was a challenge. Homeowners struggled to connect with vetted professionals, and talented artisans lacked a platform to showcase their skills.

Our mission is to bridge this gap. We've created a trusted digital marketplace that empowers both customers and service providers, making it easier than ever to bring beautiful and functional design projects to life.
